Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will my pre-paid card work with my existing mobile phone?
    A pre-paid SIM card will work in any GSM handset that is not network-locked. If your handset was subsidised by your network operator, it may be network-locked â€“ i.e. restricted to that particular cellular network. Please contact your cellular service provider or network operator and ask if your phone is network-locked. If it is locked, they may be able to unlock it. A foreign pre-paid SIM card will NOT work in a network-locked phone.
  • Do I need to have international roaming activated to use my pre-paid SIM card?
    No, you do not require international roaming! The point of purchasing a pre-paid SIM card is to avoid international roaming costs and constraints
  • How do I activate my new pre-paid SIM card?

    The SIM card manual and your handset manual will have detailed instructions on how to insert and activate your SIM card. In general, the procedure is as follows:

    • Remove the SIM card from its packaging and protective plastic frame.
    • Insert the card into your handset as indicated in the handset manual.
    • Turn on the handset.
    • You may be prompted to enter a PIN code. This code may be found on the SIM envelope or in the SIM user manual. (More about PIN and PUK codes below).
    • In a few moments your handset will acquire the mobile network and be ready for use.

    NOTE: The SIM card will only work when you are in the country for which it has been sold, and when you are within network coverage.

  • What are a PIN code and a PUK code?
    • The PIN code, or Personal Identification Number, is a private number with which to secure the use of your phone and SIM Card.
    • The default PIN code, if any, is either 1111 or 0000.
    • The PUK, or PIN Unlock Key, is another code which is used in the event of your handset being locked by the repeated entry of an incorrect PIN code.Both codes are entered into your handset as required.
    • Both the PIN code and the PUK code are included with your SIM card and should be kept safe in case they are needed. These numbers will typically be printed on the SIM envelope or in the SIM user manual.
    • For more details on PINs and PUKs, consult your handset manual.
  • What is my new phone number?

    This will displayed on the SIM card pack or envelope, and will be clearly identifiable. You may also call *111 and follow the menu prompts to "What is my phone number" and it will be announced to you. Alternatively, dial any mobile phone and your number will be displayed on the recipient's screen.

  • Can I call internationally using my pre-paid SIM card?

    Mobile Zone has negotiated preferential international rates with 012 Smile, one of Israel's leading international carriers. To access the discounted rates when calling internationally, dial 012 - country code - phone number.
    For example: to call SA, dial 012-27-82-123-4567 or 012-27-11-888-1234 where 27 is the country code; to call the US dial 012-1-555-123-4567 where 1 is the country code.

    International rates vary according to destination and time of day (peak or off-peak; holiday or work day). The deal Mobile Zone has with 012 generally offers the cheapest dialing option to most destinations. For further information on international calling via 012 Smile, please eMail support@012.net or visit www.012.net

    Note:

    • Mobile Zone is not responsible for the service provided by 012 Smile, Orange or any other third party; rates may change without notice.
    • The 012 prefix must NOT BE USED when sending SMS, which should be addressed in the usual format - e.g. +27-82-123-4567
    • You can also call internationally without using the 012 Smile service, simply by dialing the number as you normally would - e.g. +27-82-123-4567. In this case, rates are determined by Orange. More details on calling rates can be obtained from an Orange customer service representative by calling *111 and then pressing 0.

  • Does my pre-paid card provide internet browsing, E-Mail etc.?

    Yes! Mobile Zone is now supplying 3G SIMs that connect to Orange's high-speed data network. However, you may need to reconfigure your handset settings to take advantage of this feature. If you are having difficulty with this, please contact Orange customer care on *111 and they will be able to send you the new settings OTA (Over The Air).

  • How long will my pre-paid SIM card be active if it is not used?
    Your SIM card will be active for one year from date of last use. If your card has been inactive for longer than a year, the number may be suspended and the card may no longer work. Note: this period is subject to change by the local network operator, without notice.
  • How long will unused airtime remain active?
    Airtime that has not been used for six months from date of last use may be forfeited, regardless of whether incoming calls have been received during this period. Note: this period is subject to change by the local network operator, without notice.
  • Which payment methods do we accept?
    MobileZone accepts payment via PayPal, including payments made by credit or debit card. However, in the case that you don't have a PayPal account, you may still be able to pay for your purchase with a credit or debit card using PayPal's Guest Checkout service. When you are ready to check out, click the "Checkout" button. You'll notice the credit card logos next to the PayPal logo because credit card payments can also be processed through PayPal. You'll be taken to a secure page on PayPal. Look for a link below the log-in form reading Don't have a PayPal account? You'll then be prompted to fill in your billing and credit card information. You will be given the option to create a PayPal account, but you can decline that offer and continue your payment.
  • Do I need to register in order to recharge airtime?
    There is no registration process involved all you need is the mobile number you wish to recharge and a valid credit or debit card to follow our easy to use 3 Step process. Step 1 Select your network operator. Step 2 Select the airtime recharge product you would like. These come in denominations of 60, 100 and 200 Step 3 Enter your phone number Step 4 Click the Recharge button and complete payment
  • What is the maximum amount I can recharge?
    You can recharge or purchase one product every 24 hours.
  • How do I unlock my phone?
    Most cellular companies, including T-Mobile and Cingular Wireless, will unlock your phone for you if you have been an existing customer for at least 3 months. If your provider refuses to unlock your phone, there are services on ebay that will do it for you.
